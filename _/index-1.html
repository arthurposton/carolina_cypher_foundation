Who We Are

TICKET BUYERS:

The Carolina Cypher platform offers ticket buyers the opportunity to purchase tickets to incredible events and experiences by offering the same robust inventory as other ticketing sources, many times at less cost! The best part of Carolina Cypher is that every purchase you make supports a cause of your choice at no extra cost!

CORPORATE PARTNERS:

The Carolina Cypher platform provides our corporate partners the ability to donate their unused corporate tickets to events and also to provide a channel for their employees to utilize when buying tickets on their own, where it all supports their corporate community initiatives!
