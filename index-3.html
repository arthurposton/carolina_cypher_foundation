Gala

Organizing an Event of Elegance with Dinner and Dancing

Carolina Cypher Foundation is a non-profit organization striving to build community by teaching the concept of one culture and heritage. Also, it is our aims to assist the needy in the area States of North Carolina and South Carolina  contribute in any way whatsoever to the enhancement of the welfare of the citizens in the area.

Please join us for our an Elegant gala. The theme this year hasn't been chosen as of yet. It's going to be an incredible evening of food, dancing, entertainment, door prizes, and a silent auction! All proceeds will directly benefit the many charities we are involved with and the future work we hope to do with disabled veterans experiencing homelessness.

Inaugural Influencers Networking Breakfast

On February 3rd, over 250 business and community leaders from across the Greater Charlotte metropolitan area attended our Inaugural Influencers Networking Breakfast, held at the illustrious Charlotte City Club.

With special guest speaker Jeff Gelb, CIO/VP IT for Amblin Partners (formerly DreamWorks Studios), we were honored to create an environment where leaders could network with each other and discover ways in which they can make a lasting impact in their respective spheres of influence.

Lunch & Learn Events

This Luncheon is created to focus on "Introduction to Fundraising" it provides a warm handshake with the process of fundraising for nonprofit organizations. The event will be facilitated by The Carolina Cypher Foundation, a nonprofit entity and private event consulting company. The workshop will include a question and answer segment and opportunity to gain a greater understanding of what sustainable fundraising entails.
